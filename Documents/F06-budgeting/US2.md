# US2: Budget vs. Actual Variance Analysis

**As a** business owner,
**I want** to compare actual results to budget with variance displayed in absolute and percentage terms,
**So that** I can quickly see where the business is ahead of or behind plan.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A "Variance Analysis" report is available under the Budgeting module.
- [ ] The user selects a fiscal year and a date range within that year; the report covers only the months within the range.
- [ ] The report displays rows for each account with columns: Account, Actual, Budget, Variance (CHF), Variance (%).
- [ ] Variance = Actual − Budget; unfavourable variances are displayed in Signal 700 `#7E2A14`; favourable variances are displayed in Lichen 700 `#1F6040`. (Revenue under budget = unfavourable; Expense over budget = unfavourable.)
- [ ] Section subtotals are shown: Total Revenue, Total COGS, Gross Profit, Total OpEx, Net Income — each with actual, budget, and variance.
- [ ] The report is also accessible as the "Show Budget" overlay on the P&L report (FR-PL-04 / P&L US4).
- [ ] Export buttons for PDF (landscape) and CSV are available.

## Priority
Must Have

## Related Requirements
