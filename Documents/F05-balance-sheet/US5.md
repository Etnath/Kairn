# US5: Comparative Balance Sheet

**As an** accountant or business owner,
**I want** to compare Balance Sheets from two different dates side-by-side,
**So that** I can see how assets, liabilities, and equity have changed over a period.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A "Compare" toggle on the Balance Sheet report adds a second date picker labelled "Comparison Date".
- [ ] When enabled, the report displays three columns: Primary Date, Comparison Date, and Movement (Primary − Comparison).
- [ ] Positive movements on Asset accounts are shown in green; negative movements in red.
- [ ] For Liability and Equity accounts, an increase is neutral (black) and a decrease is noted but not colour-coded differently in v1.0.
- [ ] The report footer confirms that both dates individually satisfy `Assets = Liabilities + Equity`.
- [ ] Comparison columns are included in PDF and CSV exports.
