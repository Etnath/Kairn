# US4: Margin Trend Chart

**As a** business owner,
**I want** to see a line chart showing the gross margin trend for each product line over the past 12 months,
**So that** I can spot improving or deteriorating profitability before it becomes a crisis.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A "Trends" tab or section on the Margin Analysis page displays an ApexCharts line chart.
- [ ] The chart shows one line per active product line plotting gross margin % on the Y-axis and month on the X-axis.
- [ ] The default view covers the trailing 12 calendar months; a date range picker allows custom ranges.
- [ ] Each product line's trend line has a distinct colour; a legend maps colours to product line names.
- [ ] Hovering over a data point shows a tooltip with: month, product line name, revenue, COGS, and gross margin %.
- [ ] A product line can be toggled on/off by clicking its legend entry.
- [ ] The chart is responsive and renders correctly on tablet-sized screens (768px+).
