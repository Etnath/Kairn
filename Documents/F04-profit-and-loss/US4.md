# US4: Budget vs. Actual on the P&L

**As a** business owner,
**I want** to overlay budget figures on the P&L report with variance analysis,
**So that** I can see at a glance where I am overspending or underperforming relative to my financial plan.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A "Show Budget" toggle on the P&L report adds Budget and Variance columns (requires an active budget for the fiscal year).
- [ ] If no budget exists for the selected period, a message is shown: "No budget found for this period. Create one in Budgets."
- [ ] The Budget column shows the budgeted amount for each account, summed for the report date range.
- [ ] The Variance column shows: `Actual − Budget`; unfavourable variances are displayed in red.
- [ ] Percentage variance is shown alongside absolute variance.
- [ ] A summary row at the top of the report shows overall actual vs. budget net income with the total variance.
- [ ] Budget columns appear in PDF and CSV exports.
