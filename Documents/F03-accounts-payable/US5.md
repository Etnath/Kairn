# US5: AP Aging Report

**As a** business owner or bookkeeper,
**I want** to see a report of all outstanding payables grouped by how overdue they are,
**So that** I can prioritise payments and maintain good vendor relationships.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] An "AP Aging" report is available under the Accounts Payable module.
- [ ] The report is generated as of a selected date (defaults to today).
- [ ] The report shows one row per vendor with columns: vendor name, current (not yet due), 1–30 days overdue, 31–60 days, 61–90 days, 91+ days, and total outstanding.
- [ ] A totals row at the bottom sums each aging bucket.
- [ ] Clicking a vendor row expands it to show the individual bills in each bucket.
- [ ] The report can be filtered by vendor name.
- [ ] Export buttons for PDF (landscape) and CSV are available.
- [ ] Vendors with a zero outstanding balance are excluded by default; a toggle shows them.
